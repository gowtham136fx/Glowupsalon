Crafting Beauty,

Glow Up was born from a simple belief — every person deserves to feel extraordinary. Founded in the heart of Salem, we created a sanctuary where world-class artistry meets warm, personal care.

Our team of passionate stylists, skincare specialists, and wellness therapists bring years of expertise and a genuine love for their craft. We use only premium, skin-safe products and stay ahead of the latest beauty trends.

Whether stepping in for a quick trim or a full day of pampering, every visit to Glow Up is crafted to leave you looking and feeling your absolute best.
